# Welcome to Toshest 👋

**Toshest** is a cutting-edge digital ecosystem designed to seamlessly connect your life through intelligent identity, AI, and personalized applications. From finance and health to entertainment and productivity — Toshest powers it all.

---

## 🔍 What We Do

We’re building a unified, context-aware ecosystem through the following core systems:

- 🧠 **Myia** – Your personal AI assistant, always by your side
- 🪪 **Identity & OAuth Systems** – Secure access, simplified identity
- 🌐 **Shared Context Engine (SCE)** – Where all apps communicate and share meaning
- 💳 **Billing & Subscription Systems** – Transparent and reliable monetization
- 🔐 **Security & Trust Framework** – Built with privacy, protection, and resilience
- 🧰 **Developer Platform** – Hooks, APIs, and SDKs to extend the ecosystem

---

## 🚀 Our Vision

To build a secure, intelligent, and interconnected digital future that feels truly yours.

---

## 🛠 Tech Stack

We use modern technologies across all layers of our platform:

- **Languages:** JavaScript, Python, C++, TypeScript
- **Frontend:** React + Vite, Tailwind CSS
- **Backend:** Node.js, Express
- **Storage:** MongoDB, Redis, Neo4j
- **AI/ML:** Python (inference), Vosk (offline speech-to-text)
- **DevOps:** Docker, GitHub Actions
